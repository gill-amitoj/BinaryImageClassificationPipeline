Privacy & Ethical Use

Data Handling

  • Uploads are processed locally on your machine and not shared externally.
  • Images are stored in app/static/uploads/ and Grad‑CAM overlays in app/static/outputs/.
  • You can delete your results from the results page using the "Delete this result" button.
  • For production, consider auto‑deletion and HTTPS/TLS.

Explainability

  • Grad‑CAM highlights regions that influenced the prediction, but it is an approximation.
  • Use interpretability as guidance, not as absolute truth.

Acceptable Use

  • Use images you own or have permission to use. Respect licenses and privacy.
  • Do not use the system for sensitive or personal data without explicit consent and legal basis.
  • Do not deploy with harmful, discriminatory, or illegal use cases.
